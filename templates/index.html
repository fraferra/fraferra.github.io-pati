    About Me

    My name is Francesco Ferrari. I am an international student studying Electronic and Electrical Engineering with Nanotechnology at University College London (UCL). I am originally from Italy but I have been abroad since I was seventeen.

    I am currently working as an intern at the Cisco Systems headquarters in San Jose. My main occupation here is web development, specifically designing apps that run on Openstack, a cloud technology which Cisco is investing a lot in.

    I really enjoy the enterpreneurial spirit here in the Bay Area and I am trying to get involved as much as possible taking part of Startup Weekends and Hackthons. I really like the diverse and interesting pool of people that are involved in the local startup universe and tech industry environment.
